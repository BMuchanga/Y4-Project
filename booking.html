How to book a flight with Continental Airlines

Book a flight online

To find the right ticket for your trip,just enter the location you would like to travel to.This will display an new window.Enter your details and pay using your prefered method.Once done a prompt will confirm your booking.

Payment Methods

Click your prefered method of payment in the booking section and enter your details.When this is done you will receive an email containing payment instructions.Follow the instructions to complete payment.This is taken as a security measure given hacking and fraud is on the rise.Please observe caution!

Alter flight details

Once you have booked a flight,to change your flight plans you have to contact us through our email or our phone number.Doing so will connect you to one of our agents who will process your query.You may be charged a small fee(fine) for the inconvinience.
